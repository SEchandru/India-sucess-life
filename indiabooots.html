india is a country located insouth asia. it is the seventh-largest country in the world by area and the second-most populous country, with over 1.3 billion people. india is a federal parliamentary democratic republic with 28 states and eight union territories. its capital is new delhi, and its largest city is mumbai. the official languages are hindi,tamil and english, and the currency is the indian rupee. india has a rich cultural heritage and a diverse society, with seceral mojor religions including hinduism,islam,christianity. and buddhism. the country has a rapidly growing economy and is one of fastest-growing major economies in the world.

India has been home to some of the world's most successful individuals, who have made significant contributions in various fields.
